Painting

Nature painting, often referred to as landscape painting, is a genre of visual art that focuses on depicting scenes from the natural world. Nature paintings can encompass a wide range of subjects, including landscapes, seascapes, forests, mountains, rivers, wildlife, and more. Artists who specialize in nature painting aim to capture the beauty, atmosphere, and the inherent aesthetic qualities of the natural environment. This is a nature painting of a beach, done on canvas.
